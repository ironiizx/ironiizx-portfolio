Who I Am

About me

Thumbnail designer and visual artist specialized in Fortnite and UEFN.

I’m Ronen Saidman, also known as iRoniiZx. Since 2019, I’ve worked with creators and studios to develop high-impact thumbnails for Fortnite maps, events and major UEFN updates.

My workflow combines custom 3D production, AI-assisted cartoon artwork, visual direction and advanced compositing. This allows every project to use the visual style that works best for its concept instead of relying on one fixed production method.

Beyond making a thumbnail look attractive, I focus on creating visuals that communicate clearly, remain powerful at thumbnail size and adapt to Epic’s constantly evolving pre-check, similarity and Discover systems.
